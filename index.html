FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I see results?+
Most clients see ACoS improvements within 3–6 weeks of campaign restructuring. Organic listing improvements typically show in 4–8 weeks. We set clear 30-day milestones so you always know what to expect and when.
Do you lock clients into long-term contracts?+
No. We offer flexible monthly agreements. We retain clients through results, not contracts. Most of our clients have been with us for 12+ months because they see consistent, compounding growth.
I'm a UK/US seller — how does the time zone work?+
Our team covers IST which overlaps with UK morning hours. We provide async daily updates and schedule weekly calls to suit your time zone. UK and US sellers have never raised communication as an issue.
Can you manage Amazon PPC and Meta Ads together?+
Yes — and this is a real competitive advantage. Running Amazon PPC alongside Meta Ads lets us build remarketing audiences, drive external traffic, use Amazon Attribution, and execute a full-funnel strategy that standalone agencies simply can't offer.
What does your pricing look like?+
We offer fixed monthly retainers starting from ₹15,000/mo (or £150/mo for UK sellers) — no percentage-of-spend models that incentivise higher budgets over better ROAS. See our full pricing above, or book a free audit for a custom quote within 24 hours.
What makes Bitesu India different from other agencies?+
Three things: we're Amazon Verified Partners, we work across both Amazon PPC and Meta Ads under one roof, and we're obsessively focused on TaCoS and profitability — not just click metrics. We show you the actual account screenshots, not stock photos.
